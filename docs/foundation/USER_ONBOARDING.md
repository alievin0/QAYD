# USER_ONBOARDING.md

Version: 1.0
Status: Foundation
Priority: Critical

---

# Purpose

The onboarding experience should allow a company to become operational within minutes.

Users should never feel overwhelmed.

AI assists during every step.

---

# Goals

- Fast setup
- Minimal manual work
- Guided experience
- AI-assisted configuration

---

# User Journey

Step 1

Create Account

Methods

- Email
- Google
- Apple
- Microsoft

↓

Step 2

Verify Email

↓

Step 3

Choose

Create Company

or

Join Existing Company

↓

Step 4

Company Information

- Company Name
- Country
- Currency
- Language
- Fiscal Year
- Industry
- Company Size

↓

Step 5

AI Setup

AI asks:

"What type of business do you operate?"

Examples

Retail

Restaurant

Manufacturing

Medical

Construction

Education

Technology

↓

AI prepares

- Suggested Chart of Accounts
- Tax Configuration
- Financial Templates
- Reports

↓

Step 6

Invite Employees

Owner enters:

- Email
- Department
- Branch
- Role

Invitation sent automatically.

↓

Step 7

Import Existing Data (Optional)

Excel

CSV

PDF

QuickBooks

Odoo

Zoho

SAP

Oracle

↓

AI imports

Customers

Products

Invoices

Suppliers

Inventory

↓

Step 8

Connect Services

Optional

Dashboard

Every empty page should guide the user.

Never display a blank screen.

Example

"No invoices yet.

Upload your first invoice."

The average company should complete onboarding within 10 minutes.
