# Mission

Qayd is not an accounting software.

Qayd is an AI Financial Operating System that automates, supervises, and continuously improves the financial operations of businesses.

Instead of requiring humans to manually perform repetitive accounting tasks, Qayd allows AI agents to execute, review, and coordinate financial workflows while humans supervise decisions that require judgment or approval.

The mission of Qayd is simple:

> Every business should have an AI Finance Team available 24 hours a day.

---

# Vision

Build the world's most intelligent financial operating system.

A platform where accounting is no longer manual.

Instead, financial work is performed by autonomous AI agents collaborating together.

The accountant becomes a supervisor rather than a data entry operator.

Examples:

# Principles

1. AI First
Everything should be designed for AI collaboration.

2. Automation Before Manual Work
If AI can safely perform a task, it should.

3. Human Approval For Critical Decisions
Money movement always requires permissions.

4. Explain Everything
Every AI decision must be explainable.

5. Security By Design
Security is never optional.

6. Multi-Tenant Architecture
Every company is isolated.

7. Real-Time Intelligence
The platform continuously analyzes business activity.

8. Modular Architecture
Every module works independently but communicates with others.

9. Enterprise Ready
The platform should support both small businesses and large enterprises.

10. Scale Globally
Never build features limited to one country.
